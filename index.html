Guide track to listen to while you make your recording ==> Gloria Guide Track

  • Keep your headphones unplugged until after you hear the beep at the start of the guide track.
        (Your microphone will pick up the beep allowing us to syncronize everyone's recordings.)
  • Once the beep ends, plug in your headphones, so your microphone captures only your voice, not the guide track.
