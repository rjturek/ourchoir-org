Conductor Track

You'll view this video of Doug conducting as you make your recording.
  • The Conductor track file is in the Google Drive folder and is called Rutter Gloria 1st Movement Conductor Track.mp4
  • DOWNLOAD the Conductor track to your computer/phone. Do not stream it from the Google Drive folder.
  • Keep your headphones unplugged until after you hear horns at the start of the conductor track.
    (Your microphone will pick up the horns allowing us to syncronize everyone's recordings.)
  • After the horns sounds for a couple of seconds, plug in your headphones, so your microphone captures only your voice, not the conductor track.
