Conductor track to view as you make your recording ==> Rutter Gloria 1st Movement Conductor Track.mp4

  • Keep your headphones unplugged until after you hear horns at the start of the conductor track.
    (Your microphone will pick up the horns allowing us to syncronize everyone's recordings.)
  • After the horns sounds for a few seconds, plug in your headphones, so your microphone captures only your voice, not the guide track.
